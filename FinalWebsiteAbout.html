Sassan Vatanzadeh

A brief story

Sassan Vatanzadeh was once a young boy who had ambitions of helping people and providing all the support he could to his family. He grew up in Shiraz, Iran and was quite the student. Sassan loved mathematics and physics and even wanted to pursue getting a degree in math.

Soon after Sassan became 24, he decided to pursue life by moving to Canada to seek better opportunity and opened up his first business as a contractor. Sassan pursued many high contracts; however, they were all of low value and tasks that any person could perform. Sassan new deep inside he wanted to become more than just a simple task contractor and decided to employ some of his close friends and founded Square Construction.

Since then Sassan has promised to pursue only good while being afordable as he remembers how in Iran he would do all he could to help without demanding anything in return. These moral principles along other ethical codes and overal generosity, allowed young Vatanzadeh to become one of the most successful contractors in all of ontario
